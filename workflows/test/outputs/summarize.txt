**Job Title:** Vibe Coder-in-Residence (GenAI Tech EA)

**About the Role:**

The Vibe Coder-in-Residence is an engineer who will work alongside the VP of Edge AI to automate and optimize various workflows using GenAI technology. The role requires expertise in GenAI frameworks, rapid development with micro-site builders like LangChain and MCP, and storytelling.

**Key Responsibilities:**

1. Observe and Automate workflows to identify areas for improvement.
2. Create automated workflows that reduce repetition tasks.
3. Scale up workflows by composing multiple agents together.
4. Externalize Vibe's workflow to evangelize it to the team and media.
5. Develop a public demo or open-source repository for best agents.

**Daily Responsibilities:**

1. Live-code GenAI agents in Python/TypeScript.
2. Fine-tune and orchestrate LLMs using LangChain, OpenAI, and other frameworks.
3. Optimize system prompts for Vibe's agents.
4. Integrate with enterprise APIs (Teams, Confluence, Office).
5. Maintain a dashboard to track metrics on workflow adoption.

**Requirements:**

1. Experience in shipping GenAI products.
2. Mastery of GenAI and agent frameworks like LangChain, MCP, etc.
3. Rapid full-stack micro-site builder skills (e.g., Vercel).
4. Comfortable pairing-programming or pair-prompting in a fast-moving executive environment.

**Nice-to-Haves:**

1. Experience with reverse-engineering personal workflows or building AI copilots.
2. Familiarity with range of productivity hacks and coding as agents.
3. Prior startup founding engineer experience (doesn't matter if company succeeded) or hacker-in-residence cred.
4. Familiarity with enterprise security & compliance for LLM rollouts.

**Success Metrics:**

1. ≥ 5 production agents live by day 30.
2. ≥ 30% reduction in VP calendar load by day 90.
3. ≥ 2 external media features or conference demos by month 6.
